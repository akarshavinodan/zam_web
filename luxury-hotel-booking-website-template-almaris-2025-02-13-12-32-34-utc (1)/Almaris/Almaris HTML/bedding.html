Bedsheets

Bed linens not only make your room look cozier, but they also make your room look attractive. Our bed sheets are accurate in durability and flexibility. The customization of the sheets is the main attraction. These bed linens can then be removed and washed separately, making things simpler for you.

Bed Spread

Bedspreads add style to your bedroom and offer cozier, and warmer, nights of sleep. Swap them out with the seasons and choose a thicker, quilted bedspread during winter and a lighter one during summer. It's one of the easiest ways to add a little more softness (and beauty) to your life. A bedspread drapes to the floor for total coverage that fully encompasses a bed, giving it a formal look. More lightweight than a comforter, bedspreads feature traditional designs and patterns, but an increase in popularity means there's a wide variety of colours and prints available. Customize your bed with the creativity of Zamorin Decorate.

Mattress Protector

A mattress protector is an item of removable bedding that sits on top of, or encases, a mattress to protect it. Mattress protector sheets will keep your mattresses squeaky-clean and make them look new. Apart from the primary function, protectors also serve as a decorate piece. Solid colours that perfectly match your decorate will beautify your bedroom by giving it a regal touch.

Pillow & Protector

A pillow is a support of the body at rest for comfort, therapy, or decoration. Some types of pillows include throw pillows, body pillows, decorative pillows, and many more are available here at Zamorin Decorate. Pillows that aid sleeping is a form of bedding that supports the head and neck. Pillow covers extend the life of a pillow by protecting against wear and tear, but more importantly, pillow protectors provide a healthier sleep environment by blocking fluids, moisture, dust mites, bed bugs, and stains from penetrating your pillow.

Duvet & Cover

Duvets can reduce the complexity of making a bed, as they can be used without a top sheet, blankets or quilts, or other bed covers. Duvets can be made warmer than blankets without becoming heavy. The duvet itself fits into a specially made cover, usually of cotton or a cotton-polyester blend. The duvet cover can be removed and laundered as often as the bottom sheet and pillowcases. It can also add coziness to your bed.

Bed Runner

The Bed Runner (sometimes known as a Bed Throw or Bed Scarf) not only offers extra decoration to the bed but also protects the white bed linen and end of the bed from luggage, guests sitting on the bed, etc. Our offered runner is available in different sizes, finishes, and designs.
