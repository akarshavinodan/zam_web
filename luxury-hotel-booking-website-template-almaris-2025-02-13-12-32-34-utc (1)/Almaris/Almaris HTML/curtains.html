Grommet Curtain

Curtains with a grommet top have metal rings punched into the fabric that allows them to slide along the rod easily. The grommet rings create a ripple fold when you feed them onto the rod, so they form uniform pleats without training your drapes.

Tailored Pleated Curtain

Known for its waterfall pleat design, the pleats are held stiff with a buckram stiffener, allowing this type of drapery to remain very functional and easy to operate. Similar to the Ripple Fold style, our Tailored Pleat Drapery is available in many different colours, patterns, and materials. White lining is used to prevent heat and light. Variety of designs and variety in stitching is the main attraction of this kind of curtains that we provide.

Pinch Pleated Curtain

Pinch pleat curtains are a highly decorative heading, drawing together lots of fabric into a tightly gathered and stitched bunch at the top. The permanently sewn-in pleats make for a smart finish, with elegant folds flowing from top to bottom. You can have pinch pleat curtains with either a pole or a track. And these curtains are one of the classic products of Zamorin Decorate.

Pocket Rod Curtains

Rod pocket curtains, also known as tailored or pole top curtains, are minimalist-looking curtains with a sewn-in rod pocket at the top of each curtain panel. Your curtain rod simply slides into the curtain rod pocket, so these types of curtains are easy to hang up as well. Go modern with our brightly coloured cotton curtains with Rod Pockets for easy hanging and no maintenance. These Pockets of Both Curtains make it so easy to hang, while the exclusive colours give a very fresh and bold feeling that brings vibrancy indoors. The premium cotton fabric compels you to keep your hands onto the curtain, whereas the wider width of the fabric along windows and Doors looks like never before.

Goblet Curtain

Goblet pleats are a detailed heading style, similar in look to pinch pleat curtains. Tucked and folded, goblet pleats are open at the very top to give a 'goblet' or 'wineglass' look. They can be hung from a track or a pole. They are more likely to a perfect wrinkle-less collection of curtains.

Cubicle Curtain

A cubicle curtain or hospital curtain is a dividing cloth used in a medical treatment facility that provides a private enclosure for one or more patients. The curtain is usually made from inherently flame retardant (IFR) fabric and is suspended from a supporting structure or ceiling track.

Inverted Pleat Curtain

Inverted box pleated drapes have the fullness of the pleat on the back of the drape which creates a flat drapery face suitable for contemporary interior design. Box pleated drapes are typically stationary panels with clean, unbroken lines that are wonderful in silk, lightweight velvet, and everything in between.

Ripple Fold Curtain

Ripple fold style curtains are curtains that have a consistent s- curve rippling across the span of the track. The ripples are made by snaps on flat panels of fabric on the header of the curtain which gives the ripple effect to give the fullness you desire.
