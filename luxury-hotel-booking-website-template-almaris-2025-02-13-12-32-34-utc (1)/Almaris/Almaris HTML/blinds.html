Roman Blinds

We offer customers an exclusive range of roman blinds that are fabricated from quality fabric. These roman blinds are used as window decor and are made available in a variety of innovative designs as well as pattern choices. Motorized controls are also available giving you the option to open and close your blinds remotely.

Roller Blinds

Inverted box pleated drapes have the fullness of the pleat on the back of the drape which creates a flat drapery face suitable for contemporary interior design. Box pleated drapes are typically stationary panels with clean, unbroken lines that are wonderful in silk, lightweight velvet, and everything in between.

Vertical Blinds

Vertical blinds give us immense pleasure to offer our clients an unmatched range of Vertical Blinds. These are mainly used for sliding doors and large windows. Our range of blinds is available in plain as well as prints with intricate designs. These are most commonly used in both commercial and domestic sectors to enhance beautify. Vertical blinds are perfectly made by our Zamorin team and our trademark is being there for the satisfaction of our beloved customers.

Venetian Blinds

Venetian blinds make a great alternative to curtains and can create a stylish and modern look within your home. We have a fantastic range of wooden Venetian blinds and faux wood Venetians which not only bring depth and warmth to your decor but help you to take control over light and privacy levels with ease. With the experts in our business, we make it according to the taste and preference of our customers.

Panel Blinds

Panel blinds are an ideal choice for those who want to make an impact and add something a little different to their interior. With their flexible nature, not only do they make a lustrous window decoration for larger windows, bi-fold, or patio doors, but they are a fantastic choice for room dividers as well.
